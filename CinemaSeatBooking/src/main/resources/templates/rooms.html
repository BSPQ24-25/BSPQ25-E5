Our Rooms

Our cinemas have numerous rooms, each with 20 luxury seats. These are divided into 2 groups of 10 seats, separated by an illuminated staircase corridor. The rooms are equipped with the latest technology, including 4K projectors and Dolby Atmos sound systems. The seats are designed for maximum comfort, with reclining features and ample legroom.
