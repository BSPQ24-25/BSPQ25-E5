Detailed Description

Represents a movie screening event within a cinema system.

The Screening entity represents a scheduled showing of a movie in a specific room at a specific date and location. It maintains relationships with the Movie being shown, the Room where it takes place, and the Reservation entities associated with bookings for this screening.
