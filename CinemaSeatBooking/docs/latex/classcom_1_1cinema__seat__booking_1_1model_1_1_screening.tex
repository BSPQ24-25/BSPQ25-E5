\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a movie screening event within a cinema system. 

The {\ttfamily Screening} entity represents a scheduled showing of a movie in a specific room at a specific date and location. It maintains relationships with the {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1cinema__seat__booking_1_1model_1_1_movie}{Movie}} being shown, the {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1cinema__seat__booking_1_1model_1_1_room}{Room}} where it takes place, and the {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcom_1_1cinema__seat__booking_1_1model_1_1_reservation}{Reservation}} entities associated with bookings for this screening.
